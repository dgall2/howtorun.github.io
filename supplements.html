The burning sensation during intense physical activity like endurance running and sprinting is due to lactic acid buildup in muscles as a result of anaerobic resporation in a low-oxygen situation. Beta alanine, among other similar supplements, buffers the pH of the lactic acid and improves anaerobic performance capabilities.
